The Development of Machine Learning (as it relates to TensorFlow)

Cloistered Monkey

2018-05-26 13:45

Source

A Brief Timeline

Year Person Event
1763 Thomas Bayes The Underpinning of Bayes' Theorem is published
1803 Adrian-Marie Legendre Discovers the method of least squares
1894 Francis Galton Uses regression to the mean to study inherited traits
1943 McCulloch and Pitts Create the first artificial neuron model
1957 Frank Rosenblatt Creates the perceptron
1963 Vapnik and Chervonenkis Invent the Support Vector Machine algorithm
1965 Ivakhnenko and Lapa Demonstrate multilayer perceptron with non-linear activation
1980 Kunihiko Fukushima Proposes the neocognitron, a multilayer neural network for image recognition
1974 Paul Werbos Uses backpropagation to train a neural network
1982 John Hopfield Developes the Hopfield network
1986 Sejnowski and Rosenberg Develop NETtalk, an Neural Network that learned how to pronounce words
1998 Yann LeCun Trains a Convolutional Neural Network (the LeNet-5) to recognize digits
2002 Collobert, Kavukcuoglu, and Farabet Release the Torch framework
2006 Netflix Offers $1 Million prize to help with movie recommendations
2014 Ian Goodfellow, et al. Invent Generative Adversarial Networks
2015 Francis Chollet Releases Keras for deep neural network development
2015 Google Brain Releases TensorFlow
